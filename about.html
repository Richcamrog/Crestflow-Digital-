The person behind the audits

About Richard Rogers

SEO and GEO consultant. Veteran. Cyber threat analyst turned small business advocate.

Background

Richard Rogers

Richard Rogers is the founder of CrestFlow Digital, a solo consultancy based in Virginia that provides SEO and GEO audits for small businesses, with a focus on veteran-owned companies.

Before starting CrestFlow, Richard served in the United States Marine Corps as an Intelligence Analyst, then transitioned into a career in cyber threat analysis. Both roles centered on the same core skill: reading complex data, identifying patterns, and turning raw information into actionable intelligence. That background is what CrestFlow Digital is built on.

Expertise

What I specialize in

  • Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) audits for small business websites
  • Traditional SEO analysis and reporting
  • AI-assisted data analysis with human review
  • Custom AI automation and workflow integration for small businesses

He works directly with every client from intake to delivery. No account managers, no outsourced analysts, no automated reports.

The origin

Why I started CrestFlow

Richard founded CrestFlow after watching his wife's and mother-in-law's small business websites underperform in search results. Digging into the cause revealed that SEO and GEO gaps were keeping qualified customers from ever finding them. He realized the same problem was affecting small businesses everywhere, and started CrestFlow to close that gap.

The focus

Why veteran-owned businesses

While CrestFlow Digital serves any small business, Richard prioritizes veteran-owned companies. As a veteran himself, he understands the effort it takes to build a business after service and believes those businesses deserve the same visibility as better-funded competitors.

Richard is based in Virginia and works with clients nationwide.

The process

How I work

I use AI tools to pull initial data across multiple platforms, but I don't hand the wheel to a machine. I go through the results myself, figure out what the data is actually saying, and keep digging until I have a clear picture. If something looks off or incomplete, I keep going. You get a report built on judgment, not just output.

Delivery works however fits you best. Every client gets a PDF report and a private web link with the full detail. If you want to walk through it together, I am available by phone, email, or video call. The goal is a smooth handoff, feedback you can actually act on, in a format that makes sense for how you operate.

  • I review every result personally, no automated reports handed straight to you
  • Every report is tailored to your business, not a generic checklist
  • You get the PDF, the web report, and my direct line for follow-up questions
  • No handoffs, no account managers, you deal with me start to finish
  • My job is to give you the intel. Your job is to go accomplish the mission.
