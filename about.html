The origin

Why I started CrestFlow

I noticed my wife's and mother-in-law's websites were underperforming, so I dug into why. SEO and GEO turned out to be the main culprits, and I realized if their businesses were struggling with this, plenty of others were too.

The focus

Why veteran-owned businesses

I want to help all businesses, but veteran-owned businesses hit closest to home. I've seen too many of their websites fall short on SEO and GEO. Veterans have enough on their plate without worrying about whether customers can find them online. I want to make sure they get seen in both SEO and GEO searches.

The process

I built a custom program that runs your site through multiple AI systems simultaneously. Claude then assesses each result, cross-examines the findings, and keeps pushing until there is a clear fix, not just a diagnosis. You get answers, not guesswork.
