Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Who we are

CrestFlow Digital is a solo AI visibility consultancy operated by Richard Rogers, based in Virginia. Website: https://crestflow.io. Contact: Richard@crestflow.io.

What information we collect

We collect information only when you submit the contact form on this site. That form asks for:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your business website (optional)
  • Which service you are interested in
  • A description of your business and what you want to solve

We do not use cookies, tracking scripts, analytics platforms, or any third-party data collection tools. This is a static website with no advertising and no behavioral tracking of any kind.

How we use your information

The information you submit through the contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry. Specifically:

  • To follow up with you about your question or audit request
  • To review your website as part of an audit engagement, if one begins
  • To send you the deliverables you paid for

We do not add you to any mailing list, newsletter, or marketing sequence without your explicit consent.

Who we share your information with

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party. Your contact details stay between you and CrestFlow Digital.

Form submissions are processed through an n8n workflow hosted on a private server and delivered to Richard@crestflow.io. No third-party email marketing platform or CRM is involved.

How long we keep your information

Inquiry emails and any related correspondence are kept for as long as is reasonably necessary to manage the business relationship. If you would like your information deleted, email Richard@crestflow.io and it will be removed promptly.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Ask what personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent to any ongoing contact at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email Richard@crestflow.io. We will respond within 5 business days.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date. We will not retroactively change how we handle data already collected.
